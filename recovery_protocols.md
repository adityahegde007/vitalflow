# Post-Surgical Recovery Protocols

## General Guidelines
- Rest for at least 48 hours.
- Keep the incision area clean and dry.
- Take prescribed pain medication as directed.

## Symptoms to Monitor
- **NORMAL**: Mild swelling, slight bruising, low-grade fever (< 38°C).
- **CONCERNING**: Redness spreading from the incision, pus or foul-smelling discharge, fever > 38.5°C.
- **CRITICAL**: Chest pain, shortness of breath, heavy bleeding.

## Activity Levels
- Day 1-3: Minimal walking around the house.
- Day 4-7: Light activity, no lifting > 5kg.
- Day 8+: Gradual return to normal activities as tolerated.
